In today’s digital world, poor communication can lead to serious misunderstandings. For example, a student once sent a rude and unclear email to a professor regarding assignment submission. The tone of the email was informal and lacked proper structure, which created a negative impression.

This situation could have been handled better by using a polite tone, proper subject line, and clear explanation. Professional communication is important in academic and professional environments.

Using respectful language, proper formatting, and clarity helps in avoiding confusion and building a positive impression. This highlights the importance of digital etiquette in communication.
